About us

Our mission and purpose

About_us

Seamless Transitions is a funeral logistics company with an aim to make funerals as hustle free as possible throughout Kenya.

We take away the stress of planning a funeral by running all funeral errands on behalf of the bereaved family to ensure that loved ones get a befitting send off.

Seamless Transitions comes to ease the grief and hustles of funeral planning thus allowing families and loved ones of the departed a time to mourn without having to worry about the rush and hustle of pre-funeral errands, funeral arrangements and post funeral activities.

Process & Procedure

The process and procedure includes:

  • Contacting us either by phone, email, WhatsApp or an in person visit to our office.
  • You will be meet by our highly skilled team who will be able to talk to you.
  • Understanding your needs as well as your timelines
  • Planning.
  • Presentation to client.
  • Execution.

Our purpose

Our aim is to organize funerals that reflect the character and personality of the deceased.

This requires time and dedication and we are there for this and more during your time of grief.

As dedicated professionals, we are there to help and advice in whatever way we can by providing a 24-hour personal service all year through.

Seamless Transitions works in collaboration with industry players who are tried and tested over time to bring you the very best service providers offering quality and pocket friendly services across the funeral chain.
