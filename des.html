Services

At Seamless Transition we offer the following customized services to meet your needs

Transport

We have fleets comprising of new state of the art vehicles in shades of black a time honored color for funerals.

These will transport a loved one to any location within Kenya.

  • We start by establishing the place of death and then organize to have the body picked up by a team of professionals and taken to a funeral home of choice by the family.
    At this point seamless transitions may recommend a funeral home depending on the final resting location.
  • Transport of the loved one from the funeral home to the funeral service be it church or a different location as guided by the family and to the final resting place.
  • Transport for the family or special groups requiring transport on the funeral day.
Hospitality

This may take different forms depending on the family preferences.

The hospitality packages we offer include:-

  • Tenting and setting services:
    1. Tenting and seating arrangements during the mourning period at the home.
    2. Tenting on the funeral day as well as extra tenting at church or home as guided by the family.
    3. Mobile toilets.
  • Catering services.
    1. Catering services – through out the mourning period ensuring that there is continuous service of refreshments and clean up.
    2. Catering on the grave preparation day.
    3. Catering on the funeral day – ensuring that close family members get breakfast, snacks for the day and dinner.
    4. Lunch for the mourners which may be served on location or pre-packed.
Obituaries & Eulogies

Together with our partners and in consultation with the family we process the placement of obituaries in the Dailies, on local radio and TV stations as well other social media platforms.

We liaise on the Eulogy and program printing on behalf of the family, ensuring that the character and personality of the deceased is well captured as we work together with the family to come up with a life story befitting the departed.

Photography/sound/live-streaming

We work with the best in the industry to ensure that the family gets quality livestream and shots that capture the moments and keeps the memories alive.

Our partners also offer outdoor screens in instances where there are huge numbers of mourners.

We also ensure that a proper portrait photo is made for the entire period of mourning and for the funeral service.

We also make roll-up banners.

A proper sound system is essential for the memorial services and our partners have the best in the market.

Casktes

We recommend to families depending on their budget quality caskets bearing in mind that this is the most important item at the funeral.

Florals

We provide flower arrangements customized to the family's expectation.

For the period of the matanga,for church and wreathes that include:

  • Casket
  • Cross
  • Heart
  • Round shapes

Church service co-ordination

We liaise with the presiding clergy on the program, hymns, tributes, deco and all other activities to be done during the service to ensure a seamless service.

Ensure adequate seating and clean bathrooms for use by the mourners.

Counselling services

We provide you with experienced counselors for grief counselling before during and after the funeral.

Grave side/final rights

Our team is carefully selected to offer:

  • Graveside decoration services i.e. green carpets, lowering gear, canopy etc.
  • Fresh flowers – wreaths, around the casket flowers and other décore.
  • Tombstones and Epitaphs.

In most communities grave digging is done by locals for free but in instances where the families feel they would like to outsource this service we are available to organize for the same.

In instances where the final rights are at a public cemetery, we liaise with the relevant authorities to identify a site and process payment on behalf of the family.

Funeral home co-ordination

On behalf of the family we ensure:

  • That your loved ones are preserved with utmost respect and dignity.
  • That proper preservation and embalming is done.
  • That the casket and clothing are delivered to the funeral home on time and that all the paper work is done way ahead of time.
  • That washing, clothing and beautification of your loved one is done well.
