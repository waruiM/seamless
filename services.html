Services

At Seamless Transition we offer the following customized services to meet your needs

Transport

With our state of the art vehicles, we provide transport for the family to any location within kenya on the funeral day.

Florals

With our dedicated team of florists, we ensure your floral requirments to your family's customization.

Hospitality

With our dedicated team, we ensure your provided with best services to meet your needs before,on and after the funeral date.

Caskets

We provide uniquely crafted caskets to meet the family needs. Bearing in mind that this is the most important item at the funeral.

Grave side rights

We provide graveside decoration services such as lowering gear, canopy as well as green carpets which is expertly laid out.

Counselling services

We provide skilled and experienced couselors who will walk with you before during and after the funeral.

Photography

We have the best team in the industry who ensure that the family gets quality livestream and photography that capture the moments and keeps the memories alive.

Church service co-ordination

We liaise with the presiding clergy on the program, hymns, tributes, deco and all other activities to be done during the service to ensure a seamless service.

Obituary & Eulogies

Together with our partners and in consultation with the family we process the placement of obituaries in the dailies, on local radio and TV stations.

Funeral home co-ordination

We ensure that loved ones are preserved with utmost respect and dignity
